Hi, I'm Ashley.

I am currently a first-year PhD student in the Genomics and Evolutionary Dynamics team headed by Professor Trevor Graham at the Institute of Cancer Research. Prior to this, I completed an MBiochem at the University of Oxford, carrying out my Master's research project in the Oxford Protein Informatics Group.

Here, I collate some of my musings on the mechanisms underpinning life and what happens when they go wrong.
